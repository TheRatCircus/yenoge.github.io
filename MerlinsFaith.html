Project Play









Main Mechanic

you start the game with a brand new dungeon loaded at random, each time you die the room reloads in a new pattern with different enemys and items, we werent able to completely get all of the items and enemys done


















Main Information

Project Play is a game project that we had for class on with a group of 3 coders and 3 designers had to work together to make a game. We decided to make a dungeon crawler inspired by binding of Isaac and various horror pixel games. The project eventually didn't work as planned since we had to deal with someone who barely did anything and was never at school. We are thinking about continuing the game tho

















The story

In the game you play as Merlin's aprentice. Merlin tasked you to explore a cave full of magic, here in this cave you will find a sword that magically alters throughout your journey, shaping to your personality and your likings, if you manage to come out the cave with the sword you offically become a wizard
