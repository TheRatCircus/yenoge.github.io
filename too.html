Project TOO







Main Mechanic

You start the game with a flute. This flute grants you your attacks and abillities, throughout the game you can find more notes/songs that grant you new attack combos, upgrades or abillities. You can also find new flutes which are upgrades to your original, but these can break unlike your base flute. With music you can do the following things:

1. Overcome obstacles such as doors or bridges

2. Attacks and attack combos

3. Temporary upgrades

4. Interact with Npc's

5. Create your own songs for combos








Main Information

Project TOO is a game that I'm working on with a group of 4 coders and 2 designers for now, its going to be a 2d art Metroidvania platformer inspired by games such as HollowKnight, Metroid, Axiom and Ori. The games core mechanics will be that you use music to overcome enemies and obstacles. The main inspiration for the story is the original endings for fairytales and folklore. It will be singleplayer only for now and its meant to be a challenge for the player, the bosses are sort of soulslike


















Explore a vast world

Tales of old will have multiple regions/worlds that are all connected by one center realm named "The forrest realm", these connective realms are all based on different countries and their fairytales/folklore. As you progress through the game and defeat a realms boss the corruption from the boss will spread over to the main realm, making the obstacles and enemies more difficult and this makes the game more difficult as you progress to keep the player awake.
