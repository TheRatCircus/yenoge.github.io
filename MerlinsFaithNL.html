Project Play is a game project that we had for class on with a group of 3 coders and 3 designers had to work together to make a game. We decided to make a dungeon crawler inspired by binding of Isaac and various horror pixel games. The project eventually didn't work as planned since we had to deal with someone who barely did anything and was never at school. We are thinking about continuing the game tho
