Who is Lusi?

Hi there! My name is Lusi Ryborz, I'm a 19 year old student at SintLucas Eindhoven in the Netherlands. I study software development and game design. My favorite part about software development is developing games and I want to make that a part of my future as a game developer/designer. I've been coding for three years now and I have made multiple projects over the course of those years. I see game development as both a hobby and work so I do take it very seriously. The more I code the more I learn about the vast world of software. I used to play quite alot of video games so i have alot of experience with all kinds of game types and designs. My biggest inspiration are games like Dark Souls, Hollow Knight and Pokemon. I'm quite a social person and I like working with other people, although I don't mind working by myself. Being around other people and working with them just makes the process more fun to me.
