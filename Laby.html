Labyrinthine (Valko)




Main Information

Beginning of this school year i mailed Valko studios if its possible i could work on their project Labyrinthine. I started working two weeks after this email and have learned alot from it. Labyrinthine is a horror game set in multiple mazes throughout the story and the endless mode (case files). It's both a single player and multiplayer game but it will always be more fun with friends, there are tons of collectibles and secrets in the game that keep you playing.
